## Needs vs. Strategies

| Needs (Universal) | Strategies (Specific) |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| Connection | Calling my friend Sarah |
| Rest | Sleeping until noon on Saturday |
| Autonomy | Working from home |
| Nourishment | Eating at that Italian restaurant |
| Fun | Playing video games |
| Safety | Installing a security system |
| Understanding | Reading that book |
| Contribution | Volunteering at the shelter |

## The Seven Basic Needs

Marshall Rosenberg identified seven fundamental need categories:

1. **Connection** - Our need for relationships and belonging
2. **Physical Well-being** - Our bodily needs for survival and health
3. **Honesty** - Our need for authenticity and integrity
4. **Play** - Our need for joy and recreation
5. **Peace** - Our need for harmony and ease
6. **Autonomy** - Our need for choice and self-determination
7. **Meaning** - Our need for purpose and contribution

All other needs are variations of these seven.
